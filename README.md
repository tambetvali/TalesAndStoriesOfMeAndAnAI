# TalesAndStoriesOfMeAndAnAI
I am trying to open up some archetypes, motives and situations in manner of classic literature; altough as a child I was creative in this, I am not interested in writing: I have created plans for these texts, but implementations are mostly by AI. I want to present the ideas.

These stories are not based on direct realities, but on the underlying essentials and conceptions, often caricaturized or simplified. I am trying to plot the stories as they mutate from ancient history to my own recent life, and to find out the stable archetypes and even the extremes the stories cannot reach for real, given the energy and thermodynamics / mental money and karma.
